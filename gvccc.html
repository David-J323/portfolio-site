Greenwich Village Chelsea Chamber of Commerce

Client

GVCCC

Role

Jr. Graphic Designer

The Greenwich Village Chelsea Chamber of Commerce is a NP organization dedicated to aiding local businesses in the Greenwich Village, Chelsea, SoHo, NoHo, Union Square, Flatiron, East and West Village area of Manhattan. During the height of the COVID-19 Pandemic (in NYC), the Chamber wanted to assist its local businesses that were suffering from prolonged closure. To do this the GVCCC planned to: relay daily info from NYC officials, promote any re-opening businesses, and educate local businesses on how to handle the situation through webinars/online events.
